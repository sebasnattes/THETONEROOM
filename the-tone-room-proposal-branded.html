06 Our Process

How We
Work

1
Initial Creative Meeting
A virtual session to align on your vision, brand expectations, desired aesthetic, marketing goals, and target audience. This ensures every piece of content serves your brand identity.
2
Production Planning & Scheduling
We coordinate filming dates, content sessions, and a production calendar so the entire workflow is efficient and stress-free for your team.

Post-production begins immediately after filming. Content previews are shared for your approval, adjustments are made, and final deliverables are handed off on schedule.

A structured posting calendar with suggested dates and scheduling for every reel and static post — keeping your content consistent and your brand presence strong.
